Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Minoxidil

How soon will I see results?

Most users begin to notice a reduction in hair shedding within the first 2-4 weeks. Visible improvements in hair thickness and new growth typically appear after 60-90 days of consistent use. For optimal results, we recommend using Minoxidil for at least 6 months.

Is Minoxidil suitable for both men and women?

Yes, Minoxidil is formulated to work effectively for both men and women experiencing hair thinning or loss. Our formula addresses the common causes of hair loss regardless of gender.

Are there any side effects?

Minoxidil is made with natural ingredients and is generally well-tolerated. Unlike some prescription hair loss treatments, it doesn't cause sexual side effects or other serious issues. A small percentage of users may experience mild scalp tingling upon application, which is normal and indicates the formula is working.

How do I use Minoxidil?

Minoxidil is applied once daily to a clean, dry scalp. Simply use the precision applicator to apply 1ml of the solution directly to areas of thinning hair. Gently massage into the scalp and leave in. No rinsing required. For best results, apply at the same time each day.

What if it doesn't work for me?

We stand behind our product with our industry-leading money-back guarantee. If you don't see the results you're looking for within the guarantee period for your plan (30, 90, or 180 days), simply contact our customer service team for a full refund, no questions asked.
